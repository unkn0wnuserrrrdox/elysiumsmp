ABOUT US

Who We Are

ElysiumSMP is not your average survival server. We’re a high-energy, always-active Minecraft community built around survival progression, skill-based gameplay, and intense PvP moments.

We’re funny, a little chaotic, never dry, and there’s always something happening. Whether you're building, fighting, or just messing around with friends — you’ll always find action here.

This isn’t a quiet server. It’s a living world.

SERVER INFO

What the Server is About

The server focuses on three main things: Survival, Skills, and PvP.

Survival is the foundation — build bases, gather resources, and expand your world. Skills give progression and depth to gameplay. PvP brings the intensity — fights, wars, traps, and strategy-based combat.

Everything is designed to stay balanced while still allowing creativity, chaos, and competition.

SERVER RULES

Read Before Playing

PVP & Kit Rules

• Tridents and Elytras are banned in combat.
• Carts / crystals are banned in combat but allowed in traps.
• Every Tier 2 pot is allowed except Swiftness.
• Enchanted maces are fully banned. Must be named before use.
• One totem max per player.
• Two stacks of EXP max.
• No restocking mid-fight (echest or shulkers included).
• Killing naked players is allowed, but no spawn camping abuse.
• Combat logging / danger logging is not allowed.
• AFK or lag-based kills are prohibited.
• 30-minute grace period for new players.
• Running is allowed (without tridents/elytras). Pearls are allowed.
• Ask staff if anything is unclear.

──────────────────────────────

Banned Mods

• Xaero’s Minimap / World Map
• Heart Indicators
• Freecam
• Any unfair advantage mods
• Flashback allowed ONLY for cinematic use

──────────────────────────────

Unfair Advantages

Hacks, cheats, x-ray, seed cracking, and exploits are strictly banned.
Bugs must be reported immediately.

──────────────────────────────

Teams & Alliances

• Max 8 players per team
• No permanent alliances unless Founder-approved
• Must register teams in the Discord channel

──────────────────────────────

Bases & Griefing

• Griefing is allowed (except spawn)
• Stealing is allowed
• Protect your own base

──────────────────────────────

Spawn Rules

• No spawn griefing
• No killing builders at spawn
• Staff may remove disruptive builds

──────────────────────────────

Content Rules

• No stream sniping
• No content griefing
• No leaking or interfering with videos
