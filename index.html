Server Rules

PVP & Kit Rules:

• No tridents or elytras in combat
• Crystals banned in combat (allowed in traps)
• No restocking mid-fight
• Combat logging is banned
• 30 minute grace period for new players

Mods:
• No Xaero’s minimap/worldmap
• No freecam
• No cheat mods or unfair advantages

Teams:
• Max 8 players per team
• No permanent alliances without approval
