Server Rules

PVP & Kit Rules Tridents and Elytras are banned in combat. Carts / crystals are banned in combat but are allowed in traps. Every Tier 2 pot is allowed apart from Swiftness. Enchanted maces are fully banned. Your mace needs to be named before use. Any one shot tricks with mace are banned unless used in a trap. One totem carried at a time max. Two stacks of exp carried at a time max. Restocking mid-fight with your echest is banned. This also includes shulkers. Killing unarmored ("naked") players is allowed. However, do not repeatedly kill players who have just respawned or who have not had a fair opportunity to regear after dying. Combat logging (leaving during fights) is not allowed. Danger logging is also not allowed. AFK kills or lag-based kills are prohibited. New players have a 30-minute grace period upon joining. Running away is allowed (no tridents/elytras). Banned Mods: Xaero’s Minimap, Freecam, Heart Indicators. Teams max 8 players. Griefing allowed except spawn.
