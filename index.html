Server Rules

PVP & Kit Rules

Tridents and Elytras are banned in combat.
Carts / crystals are banned in combat but allowed in traps.
No mid-fight restocking (echest/shulkers).
Combat logging is not allowed.
30-minute grace period for new players.
Running away is allowed (no tridents/elytras).

Banned Mods

Xaero’s Minimap / World Map banned.
Freecam banned.
Heart indicators banned.

Teams

Max 8 players per team.
No permanent alliances unless approved.

Griefing

Allowed everywhere except spawn.
Stealing is allowed.
