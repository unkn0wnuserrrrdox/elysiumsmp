Server Rules

PVP & Kit Rules Tridents and Elytras are banned in combat. Carts / crystals are banned in combat but are allowed in traps. Every Tier 2 pot is allowed apart from Swiftness. Enchanted maces are fully banned. Your mace needs to be named before use. Any one shot tricks with mace are banned unless used in a trap. One totem carried at a time max. Two stacks of exp carried at a time max. Restocking mid-fight with your echest is banned. This also includes shulkers. Killing unarmored ("naked") players is allowed. However, do not repeatedly kill players who have just respawned or who have not had a fair opportunity to regear after dying. Combat logging (leaving during fights) is not allowed. Danger logging is also not allowed. For example, if you're in blatant danger and proceed to leave the game. This also includes abusing TPA / Home systems to teleport away from danger. AFK kills or lag-based kills are prohibited. New players have a 30-minute grace period upon joining where they cannot be attacked. To be clear, running away from fights is allowed - just without Tridents and Elytras. You may also use pearls during a fight. If you have any questions about the PvP rules, or would like any clarification, please open a ticket. ────────────────────────────────────── Banned Mods Xaero's minimap and Xaero's world map is banned to prevent unfair advantages. Heart indicator is banned. Freecam is banned to prevent abuse. Any mods that cause extreme unfair advantages are banned. If you are unsure if a mod is banned, please ask an Ownership member. Flashback is allowed for the sole purpose of cinematic shots. ────────────────────────────────────── Unfair Advantages The use of hacks, cheats, or unfair mods is strictly prohibited. This includes X-ray, minimaps, freecam/replay mods (except for recording), health indicators, seed cracking, etc. Exploiting bugs is not allowed. Any bugs must be reported through the proper channels or via a ticket. ────────────────────────────────────── Teams & Alliances Teams can have a maximum of 8 players. Permanent or ongoing alliances between teams are not allowed unless explicitly approved by a Founder. Temporary alliances between teams is only permitted during Founder approved organized wars or events. Outside of these exceptions, teams must remain independent. Teams MUST be registered in the ⁠👀│looking-for-team channel, including team name and members. ────────────────────────────────────── Bases & Griefing Griefing is allowed unless a staff member has stated otherwise. Griefing is not allowed at spawn. Keep good guard of your valuables, as they are your responsibility. Stealing is allowed regardless of whether the player is on or offline. ────────────────────────────────────── Spawn Rules As stated above, griefing is not allowed at spawn. Do not destroy builds at spawn unless permitted by an admin. Builds that disrupt spawn may be removed by admins. Do not kill players who are building or maintaining spawn. ────────────────────────────────────── Content Rules Stream sniping and content griefing are not allowed. Do not leak or interfere with another player’s content or video ideas. If you’re unsure whether a content idea is allowed, contact a Founder before proceeding.
