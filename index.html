About Us

ElysiumSMP is a fun, active Minecraft community built around chaos, PvP, survival progression, and making memories. We are always active and never boring.

About the Server

The server focuses on survival, skills, and PvP. Build, fight, explore, and grow your power while competing with others.

Server Rules

PVP & Kit Rules

Tridents and Elytras are banned in combat.
Carts / crystals are banned in combat but allowed in traps.
Every Tier 2 pot is allowed except Swiftness.
Enchanted maces are fully banned.
One totem max per player.
Two stacks of EXP max.
No restocking mid-fight (echest/shulkers included).
Combat logging is not allowed.
AFK or lag kills are prohibited.
30-minute grace period for new players.

Banned Mods

Xaero’s Minimap / World Map is banned.
Heart indicators are banned.
Freecam is banned.
Any unfair advantage mods are banned.

Teams

Max 8 players per team.
No permanent alliances without approval.

Griefing

Griefing allowed everywhere except spawn.
Stealing is allowed.
