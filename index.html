ABOUT US

Welcome to ElysiumSMP

ElysiumSMP is a community-driven Minecraft survival server built around exploration, creativity, and making memories. Whether you're a builder, fighter, trader, or explorer, there's always a place for you here.

THE SERVER

What Is Elysium About?

Our goal is to create a survival experience that feels fresh without losing the charm of vanilla Minecraft. Join events, build with friends, create towns, and become part of something bigger.

OUR TEAM

Meet The Staff

Our staff works hard every day to keep ElysiumSMP fair, enjoyable, and welcoming. We focus on helping players, hosting events, and listening to community feedback.

COMMUNITY

Everyone Has A Place

Whether you're joining with friends or starting alone, you'll always find players to build with, trade with, compete against, or simply hang out with.

RULES

Play Fair. Respect Everyone.

Treat other players with respect, avoid cheating, keep chat friendly, and help us maintain an amazing community for everyone.
