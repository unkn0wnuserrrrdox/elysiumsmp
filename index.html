About Us

Server Rules

PVP & Kit Rules

Tridents and Elytras are banned in combat.
Carts / crystals are banned in combat but are allowed in traps.
Every Tier 2 pot is allowed apart from Swiftness.
Enchanted maces are fully banned. Must be named before use.
One totem max per player.
Two stacks of EXP max.
No restocking mid-fight (echest/shulkers included).
Killing naked players is allowed (no spawn farming).
Combat logging and danger logging are banned.
TPA/home escaping during fights is not allowed.
AFK or lag-based kills are banned.
30-minute grace period for new players.
Running away is allowed (no tridents/elytras). Pearls allowed.

──────────────────────────────

Banned Mods

Xaero’s Minimap / World Map banned.
Heart indicators banned.
Freecam banned.
Any unfair advantage mods banned.
Flashback allowed ONLY for cinematic recording.

──────────────────────────────

Unfair Advantages

Hacks, cheats, x-ray, seed cracking, and exploits are strictly banned.
Bugs must be reported to staff immediately.

──────────────────────────────

Teams & Alliances

Max 8 players per team.
No permanent alliances unless Founder-approved.
Temporary alliances only during approved wars/events.
Teams must be registered in Discord.

──────────────────────────────

Bases & Griefing

Griefing is allowed except at spawn.
Stealing is allowed.
Protect your own base.

──────────────────────────────

Spawn Rules

No spawn griefing.
Do not destroy spawn builds without permission.
Staff may remove disruptive builds.
Do not kill players building at spawn.

──────────────────────────────

Content Rules

No stream sniping.
No content griefing.
Do not leak or interfere with videos.
Ask founders if unsure.
